## About Me

Justin Larghi is an actor from New Jersey with a strong passion for film, television, and storytelling. Inspired by the emotional impact that movies and media can have on people, he has spent years developing his love for performance through theater productions, Bergen County acting programs, and college coursework. Justin enjoys collaborating with casts and creative teams and is passionate about bringing authentic characters to life across stage and screen. He is especially drawn to projects that connect emotionally with audiences and leave a lasting impression.
